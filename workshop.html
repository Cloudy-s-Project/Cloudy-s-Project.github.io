Plaster Painting Workshop

A fun and creative activity where participants paint pre-molded plaster figures or shapes, such as animals, cartoon characters, or decorative items. It’s suitable for all ages and encourages artistic expression, color mixing, and hands-on creativity. Perfect for parties, school events, or relaxing art sessions!

Shirt Painting Workshop

An engaging DIY activity where participants design and paint their own t-shirts using fabric paints or markers. It encourages creativity and self-expression, allowing everyone to take home a wearable piece of art. Great for events, team-building, or artistic fun with friends!

Canvas Painting Workshop

A relaxing and creative session where participants paint on blank canvases using acrylics or watercolors. Guided or freestyle, this workshop lets individuals explore their artistic side, learn basic painting techniques, and create their own masterpiece to take home. Custom Painting Workshop

A personalized art session where participants can bring their own items such as tote bags, shoes, mugs, phone cases, or any paintable surface and decorate them using provided painting materials.
